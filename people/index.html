Eric Yakes, Managing Partner

Eric began his career in the corporate finance & restructuring group of FTI Consulting and then moved to Lion Equity Partners, a private equity buyout fund. Before founding Epoch, Eric authored The 7th Property: Bitcoin and the Monetary Revolution and has written extensively on Bitcoin and its enabling technologies. Eric double majored in Finance & Economics at Creighton University and is a CFA charter holder.

VJ Vesnaver, Operating Partner

VJ has worked as an operator, strategist, consultant, and policy expert in the Bitcoin/crypto industry since 2016. Most recently, he helped manage the $5 billion restructuring process at Celsius and led an Operations & Strategy team at Coinbase. Before joining the industry, VJ spent 8 years in Strategy and Economic consulting at BCG and FTI Consulting, and earned a JD from Harvard Law School where he launched the Harvard Law School Blockchain & Fintech Initiative and ran the Harvard Law Entrepreneurship Project. VJ earned a BA in Economics-Mathematics and Government from Colby College.

Clark Moody, Venture Partner

Clark has been a prolific builder and technologist in the Bitcoin space since 2011, creating a number of successful products in the industry, including trading platforms and industry-leading data tools. Clark founded Interchange, a tool for crypto trade reconciliation, acquired by Kraken in 2019. His RTBTC trading platform was acquired by Blockchain in 2014. Clark is a maintainer of the Rust Bitcoin library. Clark earned both his BS and MS in Aerospace Engineering at Texas A&M University.
